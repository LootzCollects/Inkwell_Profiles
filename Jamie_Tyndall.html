Biography & Influence

Jamie Tyndall is a Canadian-American comic book artist best known for his highly detailed and dynamic digital painting style, frequently featured on exclusive variant covers. With professional training from the University of Toronto and Sheridan College, Tyndall initially spent years working in media and publishing as an Art Director and Creative Director before bringing his design expertise into comics in the 2010s. He quickly gained prominence for his portrayals of strong, sexy female characters, working on covers for numerous publishers, including Zenescope, Dynamite, and IDW, for titles such as *Grimm Fairy Tales*, *Red Sonja*, *G.I. Joe*, and *Vampirella*.

Tyndall, alongside Benny Powell, co-created and publishes the successful independent series *White Widow* (2017) through their own company, Absolute Comics Group. He has also performed concept and promotional work for **FOX STUDIOS** on films like *Deadpool*, *X-Men*, and *Logan*. His career exemplifies a successful modern creator who leverages strong design sensibilities and direct-to-consumer marketing, primarily through crowdfunding platforms like Kickstarter, where his limited edition prints and covers are highly sought after by collectors.
