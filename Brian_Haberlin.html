Biography & Influence

Brian Haberlin is a multi-talented creator who has significantly contributed to the comic industry as an artist, writer, and colorist, but is arguably best known as a pioneer of digital coloring techniques. His early career saw him co-found **Image Comics' Top Cow Productions** studio, where he helped define the look of many early Image titles. He later co-founded **Anomaly Productions**, an innovative imprint focusing on high-quality print comics that integrate augmented reality elements, merging traditional comics with modern technology.

Haberlin has a deep history with some of the most prominent characters and franchises in modern comics. He is a co-creator of the successful *Witchblade* series and has worked extensively on licenses ranging from *Spawn* to *The Darkness*. His work is frequently characterized by a sharp visual style and a strong sense of digital realism, reflecting his expertise in pushing the technical limits of comic book art and production.
