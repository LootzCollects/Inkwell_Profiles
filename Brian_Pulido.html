Biography & Influence

Brian Pulido is a pioneering comic book writer and publisher, best known as the creator of the iconic supernatural anti-heroine **Lady Death** and the horror character **Evil Ernie**. Starting in the early 1990s, Pulido co-founded **Chaos! Comics**, which specialized in dark, supernatural fantasy, becoming a major force during the speculative boom of the era and helping to popularize the "Bad Girl" genre.

After the closure of Chaos! Comics, Pulido successfully transitioned his key characters to his current company, **Coffin Comics**. Utilizing crowdfunding campaigns, he has maintained a highly dedicated fanbase, proving his ability to thrive independently in the modern comic market. His business model, focusing on direct engagement and high-quality production, has allowed characters like Lady Death to experience a significant comeback and maintain a loyal following.
